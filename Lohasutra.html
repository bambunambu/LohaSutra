Questions

Frequently Asked

What materials do you work with?+
We craft with 304-grade stainless steel (rust-proof, suited for all climates) and premium mild steel (versatile, accepting any finish you desire).
How much does a commission cost?+
Pricing reflects size, material, and complexity. Nameplates begin at ₹800, wall art at ₹1,200, installations at ₹3,000. Contact us for a precise quote.
What dimensions can you achieve?+
Single pieces up to 4 feet by 4 feet. For larger visions, we create modular sections that unite seamlessly.
Do you deliver outside Kerala?+
Yes. Pan-India delivery. Each piece arrives within 7–10 days, carefully packed.
Can I provide my own design?+
Absolutely. Sketches, images, or references — all inspiration welcomed. We refine it with your approval before execution.
What finishes are available?+
Stainless steel: brushed, mirror, or satin. Mild steel: raw, powder coat (custom colors), or hand-applied patinas.
Do you assist with installation?+
Within Thrissur, we arrange installation. For other locations, detailed instructions and mounting hardware are included.
What payment methods do you accept?+
UPI, bank transfer, cards, digital wallets, or cash on delivery (select commissions). 50% advance to begin, balance before dispatch.
